Getting There

By Car

2 Fern Road, Hatfield, Harare. Tap below to open directions in Google Maps.

Get Directions
By Public Transport (Kombi)
  1. From town, board a Kombi heading to Hatfield Kilwinning at the Charge Office.
  2. Drop off at the Tuckshops — this is the final stop for the Kombi.
  3. Turn left where you see the car wash and Musika, onto Alexandra Road.
  4. Follow Alexandra Road until the first right turn, onto Fern Road.
  5. Walk a few metres ahead — the venue is on your right, just after Mumvuriwedombo Gardens.
